About Suffah Official

Our Mission & Vision

Suffah Official was established with a single purpose: to make authentic Islamic knowledge accessible to every Muslim, regardless of their location. Named after the Ashab-us-Suffah — the companions who dedicated themselves to knowledge at the Prophet's ﷺ mosque — we carry that spirit into the digital age.

We believe that Islamic education is an obligation, not a luxury. Our team of qualified scholars, Huffaz, and teachers work tirelessly to provide structured, verified, and spiritually enriching programs for students of all ages and backgrounds.

Every course at Suffah Official is grounded in the Quran, Sunnah, and the understanding of the pious predecessors (Salaf as-Salih). We do not compromise on authenticity.
