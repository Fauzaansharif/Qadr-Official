About Us

About Qadr Academy

Assalamualaikum warahmatullah wabarakatuh Qadr Academy is a global e-learning platform dedicated to teaching Nazrah Quran with proper Tajweed and spreading Islamic knowledge. Along with Quranic recitation, we offer courses on Hadees, Fiqh, Seerah, and other essential Islamic studies.


Our mission is to make high-quality Islamic education accessible to every Muslim, anywhere in the world. With experienced instructors, interactive tools, and comprehensive courses, we aim to help learners strengthen their connection with Allah and live according to the teachings of Islam. Join us on this journey of knowledge and spiritual growth.
