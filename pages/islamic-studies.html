Course Modules

What You Will Learn

🕌

Aqeedah — Islamic Creed

The six pillars of Iman: belief in Allah, His Angels, His Books, His Prophets, the Last Day, and Divine Decree (Qadar). Understanding Tawheed (monotheism) and its categories.

🤲

Five Pillars of Islam

Shahada, Salah, Zakah, Sawm, and Hajj — their importance, conditions, and how to fulfill them correctly in everyday life according to the Sunnah.

📿

Essential Du'as

Daily supplications: morning and evening adhkar, du'as for eating, sleeping, entering the home, and more. Memorizing and understanding these transforms daily life into worship.

🌱

Islamic Morals & Akhlaq

Truthfulness, humility, patience, gratitude, kindness to parents, and respecting neighbors — the character qualities every Muslim must cultivate in daily life.

👨‍👩‍👧

Family & Social Etiquette

Rights of parents, rights of children, rights of neighbors, Islamic manners in eating, greeting, and interacting — social Islam in practice.

🌙

Islamic History Basics

Brief but impactful lessons on the major prophets, the four rightly guided Caliphs, and the golden era of Islamic civilization to inspire identity and pride.

📖

Quran & Its Importance

Understanding the Quran as guidance, the importance of reciting it daily, its benefits, and how to approach it with proper respect and understanding.

⚡

Halal & Haram Basics

Key prohibitions and permissions in Islam — food, entertainment, financial dealings, and interactions — to help students navigate modern life Islamically.

Course Levels

Structured in Three Levels

1

Level 1 — Foundations

For children aged 7–12. Basic pillars, du'as, Kalimah, and Islamic manners. Interactive and story-based approach.

2

Level 2 — Intermediate

For youth aged 13–18. Deeper Aqeedah, social ethics, Islamic history, halal/haram, and strengthening identity.

3

Level 3 — Advanced

For adults. Complete Aqeedah study, contemporary Islamic issues, spirituality (Tazkiyah), and Islamic philosophy.
