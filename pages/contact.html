FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Are classes conducted live or pre-recorded?

All classes are pre-recorded but alternate days live doubt solving sessions are conducted via Zoom or Google Meet, allowing students to ask questions in real time.

What is the minimum age for Hifz?

We recommend age 7 and above for Hifz. However, each student is assessed individually for readiness and aptitude.

Is there a free trial class?

Yes! We offer one free trial class so you can experience the teaching style and meet your teacher before enrolling.

What countries do you accept students from?

We accept students from anywhere in the world. Classes are scheduled to suit your time zone wherever possible.

Do you provide certificates?

Yes, official completion certificates are awarded upon successfully finishing each course. Hifz students receive a special certificate.
